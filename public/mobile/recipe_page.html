Chicken Rice
  • Description
  • Comments (3)
Chicken rice menu that is easy and simple to make
Detail
  • Prep Time: 15M
  • Cook Time: 3H
  • Total Time: 3H15M
  • Total Calorie: 140 cals
  • serving: 10
Ingredients
  • 10 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/8 teaspoon celery salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/3 cup dry sherry
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
Directions
  • Make the Chile Sauce: Combine all the ingredients in a bowl, mix well, and season with salt, to taste. Set aside
  • Make the Chicken and Rice: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil (enough water to immerse the chicken). Add the chicken to the boiling water. Lower the heat to a simmer just under boiling point and cook, covered, until just cooked through, about 40 minutes.
  • Heat the oil in a wok or saucepan over high heat. Add the garlic and stir-fry until fragrant. Add the rice and stir-fry for 1 to 2 minutes.
  • Whisk 1/4 cup of the hot chicken stock into the chile sauce.
  • Arrange the rice and poached chicken on a platter.
